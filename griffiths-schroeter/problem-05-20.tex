\FBOX{
5.20
Find the average energy per free electron ($E_{\rm tot}/Nd$), as a fraction of the
Fermi energy. {\it Answer:} $(3/5)E_F$.
}

From equation (5.56)
\begin{equation*}
E_{\rm tot}=\frac{\hbar^2\left(3\pi^2Nd\right)^{5/3}}{10\pi^2mV^{2/3}}
\end{equation*}

From equations (5.54) and (5.53)
\begin{equation*}
E_F=\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}\left(\frac{3\pi^2Nd}{V}\right)^{2/3}
\end{equation*}

Hence
\begin{equation*}
\frac{E_{\rm tot}/(Nd)}{E_F}=\frac{3}{5}
\end{equation*}
