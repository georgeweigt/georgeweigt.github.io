\section*{Component arithmetic}

Tensor plus scalar adds scalar to each tensor component.

{\color{blue}
\begin{verbatim}
A = ((a,b),(c,d))
A + 10
\end{verbatim}}

$\displaystyle
\begin{bmatrix}
a+10 & b+10
\\[1ex]
c+10 & d+10
\end{bmatrix}
$

\bigskip
The product of two tensors is the Hadamard (element-wise) product.

{\color{blue}
\begin{verbatim}
A = ((a,b),(c,d))
A A
\end{verbatim}}

$\displaystyle
\begin{bmatrix}
a^2 & b^2
\\[1ex]
c^2 & d^2
\end{bmatrix}
$

\bigskip

Tensor raised to a power raises each component to the power.

{\color{blue}
\begin{verbatim}
A = ((a,b),(c,d))
A^2
\end{verbatim}}

$\displaystyle
\begin{bmatrix}
a^2 & b^2
\\[1ex]
c^2 & d^2
\end{bmatrix}
$
