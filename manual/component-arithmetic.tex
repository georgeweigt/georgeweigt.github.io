\section*{Component arithmetic}

Tensor plus scalar adds the scalar to each tensor component.

{\color{blue}
\begin{verbatim}
(x,y,z) + 10
\end{verbatim}}

$\displaystyle
\begin{bmatrix}
x+10
\\[1ex]
y+10
\\[1ex]
z+10
\end{bmatrix}
$

\bigskip
The product of two tensors is the Hadamard (element-wise) product.

{\color{blue}
\begin{verbatim}
A = ((1,2),(3,4))
B = ((a,b),(c,d))
A B
\end{verbatim}}

$\displaystyle
\begin{bmatrix}
a & 2b
\\[1ex]
3c & 4d
\end{bmatrix}
$

\bigskip

Tensor raised to a power raises each component to the power.

{\color{blue}
\begin{verbatim}
(x,y,z)^2
\end{verbatim}}

$\displaystyle
\begin{bmatrix}
x^2
\\[1ex]
y^2
\\[1ex]
z^2
\end{bmatrix}
$
