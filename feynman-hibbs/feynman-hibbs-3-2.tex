3-2.
Given the free particle kernel
\begin{equation*}
K_0(b,a)=\left(\frac{m}{2\pi i\hbar(t_b-t_a)}\right)^{1/2}
\exp\left(\frac{im(x_b-x_a)^2}{2\hbar(t_b-t_a)}\right)
\end{equation*}

show that
\begin{equation*}
\frac{\partial K_0}{\partial t_b}
=-\frac{i}{\hbar}
\left(
-\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}
\frac{\partial^2 K_0}{x_b^2}
\right)
\end{equation*}
