that this expression using complex variables is indeed
correct (cf. problem 8-4).

\Phi_0=\exp\left(
-\sum_{\mathbf k}
\frac{kc}{2\hbar}

\bar a_{1,\mathbf k}^*
\bar a_{1,\mathbf k}
+
\bar a_{2,\mathbf k}^*
\bar a_{2,\mathbf k}
\right)
\right)
\tag{9.43}
\end{equation*}
