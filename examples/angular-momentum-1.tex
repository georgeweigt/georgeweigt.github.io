\section*{Angular momentum 1}

Let $\mathbf L\psi$ be the angular momentum of wavefunction $\psi$
where $\mathbf L$ is the operator
\begin{equation*}
\mathbf L=\mathbf r\times\mathbf p
=\begin{pmatrix}L_x\\L_y\\L_z\end{pmatrix}
\end{equation*}

From the cross product we have
\begin{align*}
L_x&=yp_z-zp_y
\\
L_y&=zp_x-xp_z
\\
L_z&=xp_y-yp_x
\end{align*}

Verify the commutation relation
\begin{equation*}
\mathbf L\times\mathbf L=i\hbar\mathbf L
\end{equation*}

which in component form is
\begin{align*}
[L_y,L_z]&=i\hbar L_x
\\
[L_z,L_x]&=i\hbar L_y
\\
[L_x,L_y]&=i\hbar L_z
\end{align*}

Let $L^2$ be the magnitude squared of $\mathbf L$.
\begin{equation*}
L^2=|\mathbf L|^2={L_x}^2+{L_y}^2+{L_z}^2
\end{equation*}

Verify the commutation relations
\begin{equation*}
[L_x,L^2]=[L_y,L^2]=[L_z,L^2]=0
\end{equation*}
