\section*{Addition of angular momentum 3}

Let $\Psi$ be an eigenstate of $J^2$.
Then for total angular momentum quantum number $j$
\begin{equation*}
J^2\Psi=j(j+1)\hbar^2\Psi
\end{equation*}

The following $\Psi$ are eigenstates with $j=l+\frac{1}{2}$.
\begin{align*}
\Psi&=Y_{l,l}\chi_+
\\
\Psi&=Y_{l,-l}\chi_-
\end{align*}

For $m<l$ the following linear combinations are eigenstates.
\begin{align*}
\Psi&=\left(\frac{l+m+1}{2l+1}\right)^{1/2}Y_{lm}\chi_+
+\left(\frac{l-m}{2l+1}\right)^{1/2}Y_{l,m+1}\chi_-
& j&=l+\tfrac{1}{2}
\\
\Psi&=\left(\frac{l-m}{2l+1}\right)^{1/2}Y_{lm}\chi_+
-\left(\frac{l+m+1}{2l+1}\right)^{1/2}Y_{l,m+1}\chi_-
& j&=l-\tfrac{1}{2}
\end{align*}
