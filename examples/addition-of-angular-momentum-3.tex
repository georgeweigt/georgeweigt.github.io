\section*{Addition of angular momentum 3}

Let $\Psi=Y_{lm}\chi_\pm$ be an eigenstate of $J^2$.
Then for $j=l+\frac{1}{2}$
\begin{equation*}
J^2\Psi=j(j+1)\hbar^2\Psi
\end{equation*}

The following $\Psi$ are eigenstates of $J^2$.
\begin{align*}
\Psi&=Y_{l,l}\chi_+\tag{1}
\\
\Psi&=Y_{l,-l}\chi_-\tag{2}
\end{align*}

For $-l<m<l$ the following $\Psi$ are also eigenstates of $J^2$.
\begin{equation*}
\Psi=\left(\frac{l+m+1}{2l+1}\right)^{1/2}Y_{lm}\chi_+
+\left(\frac{l-m}{2l+1}\right)^{1/2}Y_{l,m+1}\chi_-
\tag{3}
\end{equation*}
